Understanding Flight Delays

Flight delays are an unfortunate but common part of air travel. While they can be frustrating, delays are often caused by factors designed to keep passengers safe and flights operating efficiently. Knowing why delays happen—and what you can do about them—can help reduce stress and improve your travel experience.
