What Passengers Can Do During a Delay?

  1. Stay Informed
    • Use airline apps, airport screens, and notifications to track updates in real time.
  2. Know Your Rights
    • Passenger protections differ by country and airline. Understanding these policies can help you advocate for rebooking or compensation when eligible.
  3. Act Early
    • If a delay looks significant, speak with a gate agent or use the airline app to explore alternative flights before options fill up.
  4. Pack Smart
    • Keep essentials like chargers, medications, and snacks in your carry-on in case of long waits.
